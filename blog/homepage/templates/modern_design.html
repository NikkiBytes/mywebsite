Hey! I am a computer scientist with a focus in bioinformatics. I specialize in statistical computing and machine learning. Using artifical intelligence I believe we can find solutions in science, as well as answer and model other great questions. Through research and application of scienfitic computing I seek to find, model and understand patterns.

I have always thought and approached situations in a modular fashion and feel that computer science found me. With an interest in nature, logic and computation, the fields of computer science and bioinformatics have felt like a natural fit for me.

I consider myself an existentialist in that I emphaize personal existence, freedom and choice. Thererefore I think we have to define our own meaning and try to make rational decisions and as a product of this I place a lot of responisibility in myself and continue to work on self-improvement.

I also enjoy reading, playing games, philosophy, riding horses, acting and being around animals and in nature. Thanks for coming to my page.
